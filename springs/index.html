RVF: manufacturer of springs for 30 years...

Our business is specifically devoted to designing and manufacturing compression springs, torsion springs and traction springs.

For over thirty years, RVF have been specializing in the manufacture of 0.1 mm to 2 mm cold-rolled springs of all types. The springs are manufactured using drawn round wire made of carbon steel, stainless steel, alloy steel, copper or brass.

RVF assists you in designing the spring with ideal optimal but which remains within your budget.

An industrial experience

With a plant comprising 55 machines operated by teams working year-round, RVF can fulfil almost any requirements for 0.1 mm to 2 mm wire springs, in addition to specific conditioning or pre-assemblies.

In order to minimize the constraints involved in forming the wire, all of our machines are equipped with a conveyor furnace.

We have a grinding and compression unit to ensure optimal perpendicularity of the spring elements.

Ressorts RVF has extended its offer to include “Clean” springs thanks to its new cleaning department equipped with a conditioning system adapted to the specific requirements of your project leaders. (springs mounted on plates, springs mounted on rods, springs placed in tubes, or tubes simply placed in bags).
